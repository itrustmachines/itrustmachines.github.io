MediaTek Makes Its First-Ever Investment In A Blockchain Startup

International Trust Machines Corporation (ITM) announced that it has received a pre-A round fund raising from the global IC design house MediaTek Inc., and the funds will be used to develop a new generation of blockchain scaling IC technology, related industrial applications and global expansion.

ITM was established in 2019. Since establishment and has won several awards such as Meet Taipei 2019 and QITC2019. In addition, a partnership project from ITM has recently been shortlisted for the Global Mobile Awards of MWC, the world's largest wireless communication exhibition 2020. At this stage, ITM has been collaborated with well-known enterprises, such as Microsoft, Qualcomm, Taiwan Mobile, etc.

Julian Chen, CEO and co-founder of ITM, said that ITM focuses on the integration of blockchain and the Internet of Things. The patented blockchain SDK, which contains unique algorithm, is only 100KB and can be easily ported on low-level IoT chips. A 32 Bytes ledger fingerprint can seal one million transactions, and can quickly audit selected data. It is a leading global IoT blockchain solution with both efficiency and cost.

ITM's blockchain solution from the chip level enables an effective integration of software and hardware, creating new opportunities for data security protection of the Internet of Things. MediaTek has long been in alignment with government’s goal to invest new and innovative industries, injecting capital and vibe to assist the development of internationally competitive Taiwanese startups. The endeavor will not only drive Taiwan ’s economic growth, but also look forward to creating more Taiwan ’s new stars in the international market.
