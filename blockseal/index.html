Attest / Verify Data

Attest files in any format at any location

Intuitive UI makes for easy uploads and easy attestations of selected files the creation of crypto evidence (a unique hash to prove the specific look and content of a document upon sealing) with a root hash from any digital document that gets registered to the blockchain.

Establish immutable trust on the blockchain

A public blockchain distributed ledger mechanism makes for a decentralized solution that provide the highest possible levels of security, efficiency, and compliance.

Eliminate the risk of leaked data

Original data remains at client’s secure site, only document hash gets stored on the blockchain. Zero risk of leaks to confidential or sensitive data

Compliance with GDPR

Only root hash stored on blockhain for cross-validation only and cannot ever be derived back to raw data. BlockSeal’s method for fingerprint attestation that keeps sensitive data in client’s original secured database is fully compliant with GDPR.

Transparent open-source verification

An open-source verification program published on the ITM website can validate selected data to determine whether original or manipulated. Furthermore, root hashes on the blockchain are always publicly available.

Hyper-scale data supported with cost advantages

ITM provides cutting-edge solutions capable of attesting million-scale data with one 32-byte crypto proof. Through the scaling solution, ITM can effectively enhance blockchain efficiency and reduce blockchain operation costs.
